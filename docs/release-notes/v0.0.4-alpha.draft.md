# v0.0.4-alpha

v0.0.4-alpha is the next prepared alpha release line after the frozen
v0.0.3-alpha artifacts. It is an alpha substrate hardening release candidate,
not a production, compliance, or legal-certification release.

## Scope

- Restores the FreeTSA timestamp verifier path on `main` after the v0.0.3-alpha
  tag. This is a verifier interoperability fix for the next alpha artifacts,
  not a retag of v0.0.3-alpha.
- Adds the EU AI Act Article 12 aligned profile document with conservative
  boundaries: the profile maps logging surfaces and evidence references, but it
  does not claim Article 12 compliance certification.
- Documents verifier independence: the trust root is the open-source
  deterministic verifier plus versioned schemas and reproducible evidence, not
  a closed API or hosted service.
- Adds the retention/deletion proof ADR for commit-then-redact handling,
  minimization, retention markers, and deletion-proof references. It does not
  claim GDPR compliance.
- Keeps `attestplane verify` scoped to chain/report-oriented verification with
  ProofBundle metadata and `policy_trace_refs` closure checks.

## Explicit Non-Claims

- Not production-ready.
- Not compliance-ready.
- Not legal advice.
- Not an EU AI Act conformity assessment.
- Not GDPR compliance certification.
- Not a full ProofBundle, signed, anchored, or compliance certification
  verifier.
- Does not change the frozen v0.0.3-alpha tag or published v0.0.3-alpha
  registry artifacts.
