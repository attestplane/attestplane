## Context

What is the situation that is motivating this decision? Include relevant constraints, prior decisions, regulatory drivers, and the alternatives that were seriously considered. Be specific about what is unknown or assumed.

If this ADR is responding to a specific compliance framework requirement, cite the article and version (e.g., "EU AI Act Article 12(2)(a), final text as of 2026-05-17").

## Decision

State the decision in active voice and imperative form. One paragraph if possible. Avoid hedging — the decision itself should be unambiguous; uncertainty belongs in Context or Consequences.

## Consequences

What becomes easier as a result of this decision?

What becomes harder or impossible as a result of this decision?

What follow-up work does this decision create? List the issues or ADRs that this decision blocks or unblocks.

## Alternatives considered

Briefly describe each alternative that was seriously evaluated and the reason it was not chosen. "None considered" is rarely an honest answer; if it is the answer, say so explicitly and explain why no alternatives existed.

## Compliance and audit notes
