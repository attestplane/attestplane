------------------------------------------------------------------------

How to sign off your commits in Attestplane:

    git commit -s -m "your commit message"

This appends a "Signed-off-by" trailer using your `user.name` and
`user.email` from your git config. By doing so, you certify the four
clauses above.

All pull requests merged into `main` must have every commit signed off.
A CI check enforces this. To retroactively sign off a series of commits:

    git rebase --signoff <base-branch>

For background on why DCO and not CLA, see GOVERNANCE.md §
"License & Re-License Policy".
